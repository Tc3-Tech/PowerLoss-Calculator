Calculation Formulas

Current: I = P ÷ V
Power Loss: Ploss = I² × R
Wire Temperature: T = Tambient + (Ploss × Rthermal)
Efficiency: η = (P - Ploss) ÷ P × 100%
Resistance: R = (ρ/1000ft) × Length
